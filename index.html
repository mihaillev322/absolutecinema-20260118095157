What We Offer

Absolute Cinema specializes in comprehensive home theatre installation services designed for Canadian homes and condos. Our expert technicians handle everything from basic TV mounting to complex Dolby Atmos surround sound systems.

TV Mounting Service

Professional wall mounting for all TV sizes with proper cable management and wall stud detection. We ensure your display is perfectly positioned and securely mounted according to Canadian building standards.

Surround Sound Installation

Complete audio system setup including 5.1, 7.1, and Dolby Atmos configurations. Our technicians handle speaker placement, receiver calibration, and acoustic optimization for your specific room layout.

Projector Installation

Cinema-grade projector mounting and screen installation with proper throw distance calculation and ambient light control. Perfect for basement theatres and dedicated media rooms.

In-Wall Speaker Installation

Discreet speaker integration with professional-grade in-wall and in-ceiling options. All wiring meets Canadian electrical code requirements with clean, hidden cable runs.

Home Theatre Calibration

Precision audio and video calibration using professional equipment to optimize your system's performance. Includes room correction, color accuracy, and sound field tuning.

Smart Home Integration

Seamless connectivity with existing smart home systems, streaming devices, and automation platforms. Compatible with popular Canadian internet and cable providers.

How It Works

Our streamlined installation process ensures your home theatre project is completed efficiently and to the highest standards. We handle every detail from planning to final calibration.

1

Consultation & Assessment

In-home visit to assess your space, discuss your entertainment goals, and provide detailed recommendations. We measure rooms, check electrical access, and identify any condo or building restrictions.

2

Custom Design & Quote

Detailed installation plan with equipment recommendations, cable routing diagrams, and transparent pricing. We ensure all work complies with local building codes and condo regulations.

3

Professional Installation

Certified technicians handle all mounting, wiring, and equipment setup. We protect your home during installation and clean up thoroughly when complete.

4

Calibration & Training

Precision system calibration for optimal audio and video performance, followed by comprehensive training on operating your new home theatre system.
